Smallest Enclosing Circle Visualization

Welcome to the Smallest Enclosing Circle Visualization tool!

Instructions:

Click on the canvas to add points. Adjust the delay between frames using the input field below.

The color of points indicates the following:

  • Blue: Points that are not being processed at the current step
  • Green: The Point that is being currently processed as a part of the Outermost Loop
  • Magenta: The Point that is being currently processed as a part of the Loop at the second level
  • Yellow: The Point that is being currently processed as a part of the Innermost Loop
  • Red: Points that are pinned on the boundary of the current circle

If the point being processed at the current step has dashed lines around it, it means that the point lies outside the current circle.
